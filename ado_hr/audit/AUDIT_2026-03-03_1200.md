## 5. New Issues Identified

### Issue N1: Scope Creep — Mid-Sprint Addition (HIGH)

**Issue:** A new Feature (#200059 "Hire Sales & Mktg. for JIT") was created **today (March 3)** — Day 9 of a 14-day iteration — with 4 child stories immediately set to Active.
**SAFe Standard:** Once an iteration is planned, no new scope should be added unless existing work is removed. New work should go to the Product Backlog for the next iteration.
**Impact:** Adds 4 items to an already overloaded iteration. Increases the risk of carryover for existing commitments.
**Recommendation:** Move the 4 Sales & Marketing stories to Iteration 6.5 backlog unless the team explicitly agrees to de-commit from other items.

### Issue N2: Potential Duplicate Story (MEDIUM)

**Issue:** Story #198684 ("Create acknowledgment tracking system") appears to be a **duplicate** of #198683 (same title, same parent Feature #197229). Story #198683 is now Closed, while #198684 is New.
**SAFe Standard:** Each story should be unique and independently valuable.
**Recommendation:** Verify if #198684 is a legitimate follow-up or an accidental duplicate. If duplicate, remove it.

### Issue N3: Completed Feature Not Closed (MEDIUM)

**Issue:** Feature #197685 ("Hire Bubble Developer") has **all 3 child stories Closed**, but the Feature itself is still in "Active" state.
**SAFe Standard:** Features should be closed when all child stories are complete.
**Recommendation:** Close Feature #197685. Similarly, review Feature #198202 ("Offboarding of Ike Lloyd Yana") — all 5 child stories are Closed.
