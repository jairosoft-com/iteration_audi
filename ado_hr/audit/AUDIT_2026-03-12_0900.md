### 10.5 Key Patterns — Cross-Audit Learnings (10 Audits)

The root cause is reactive recruitment: each new applicant triggers an immediate sprint item rather than a queue. A **rolling recruitment queue** (add to backlog, activate one at a time) would resolve this.

**Pattern 2 (Emerging): Late-Sprint Batch Delivery**
Both 6.4 and 6.5 show a pattern of high Active items early in the sprint with very few closures until the final days. While this may reflect the nature of HR work (evaluations require external parties, LinkedIn campaigns accumulate responses over time), it creates high sprint failure risk. SAFe recommends incrementally working toward "Done" rather than waiting for all items to mature simultaneously.

Recommendation compliance jumped from 0% (Day 2) to 22% (Day 3). The team acted on two recommendations: no new scope was added, and #195671 was finally resolved. This shows increasing receptiveness to governance feedback — a healthy trend.
