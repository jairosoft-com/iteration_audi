### 4.2 New Finding: N9 — Delivery Stall (CRITICAL)

**Severity: Critical**

**Issue:** Zero stories have been closed in 5 sprint days (Mar 11–16). The board state is completely frozen — identical state distribution (11 New, 11 Active, 0 Closed) on Day 2 and Day 5. This is the longest delivery stall observed in the 10-audit series.

**SAFe Standard:** Teams should demonstrate continuous flow with stories completing regularly throughout the iteration. A mid-sprint health check should show measurable progress toward the iteration commitment.

**Impact:** With 0 of 41 SP burned at the midpoint, the sprint is at severe risk of failing its commitment. Even aggressive delivery in the final 5 days cannot recover the full backlog. The 11 Active items appear to be "stuck in progress" — started but not finishing.

**Root Cause Hypothesis:** The 11 simultaneous Active items create a context-switching burden that prevents any single item from reaching completion. SAFe's "Stop starting, start finishing" principle is being violated. Combined with Almera handling all 22 items solo, the WIP overload is causing a delivery bottleneck.

**Recommendation:** Immediately reduce Active WIP to 2-3 items. Focus on closing the 6 S&M recruitment items first (they share a common workflow), then move sequentially through remaining Active items.
