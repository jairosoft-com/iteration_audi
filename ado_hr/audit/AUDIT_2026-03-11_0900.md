### 9.4 Key Patterns and Learnings

**Pattern 1 (Recurring): S&M Hiring Drives Scope Creep**
Both Iterations 6.4 and 6.5 experienced mid-sprint scope additions driven by Sales & Marketing hiring. The team treats each new applicant as a sprint emergency. A process fix is needed: new applicants should be added to the backlog and pulled into the next sprint unless there's a documented business urgency.

**Pattern 2 (Positive): Audit Feedback Being Internalized**
The APE target date correction (Mar 16 → Mar 19) shows the team reads and acts on audit recommendations, at least selectively. Quality compliance (AC, INVEST, SP) remains at 100% even for rush additions.

**Pattern 3 (Concerning): Recommendation Compliance Declining**
Day 1 recommendations had 0% compliance on Day 2. By comparison, earlier in the series (6.4), recommendations would see partial compliance within 2–3 days. The team may be overwhelmed and unable to address governance while handling active work.

**Pattern 4 (Structural): Bus Factor Compounds All Risks**
Every risk on the heat map is amplified by F3 (Bus Factor = 1). The WIP explosion, scope creep, and unrealistic deadlines would be manageable with 2–3 team members but are critical for a solo contributor.
