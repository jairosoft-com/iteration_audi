## 1. Executive Summary

This is the **first audit of Iteration 6.5**, a new two-week sprint that began today (March 9, 2026). Iteration 6.4 ended on March 8, 2026. This audit establishes the Day 1 baseline for Iteration 6.5 and assesses the transition from the previous iteration.

**Critical Transition Issues Identified:**

- **7 User Stories** are in Iteration 6.5 with an estimated **12+ Story Points** committed (excluding the story with no SP assigned).

**Overall Status: CAUTION — New iteration starts with unresolved technical debt from 6.4, reduced capacity, and incomplete refinement on several new stories.**

**Total stranded: ~30 Story Points of unfinished work.**

Per SAFe, at iteration boundaries, all incomplete work must be explicitly dispositioned: either moved to the next iteration (if re-committed), returned to the Program Backlog for re-prioritization, or closed/removed if no longer relevant. **Recommended Actions:**

2. Items that are still needed should be moved to Iteration 6.5 (if capacity allows) or placed in the backlog.
3. Items with breached deadlines (#199524, #199578) need AC updates with revised dates.
4. #200069 should be moved to the backlog with a requirement to meet DoR before re-commitment.
5. #199353 (Cross Training) must have Description and AC added — it has been non-compliant for **6 consecutive audits**.

### Finding 15: 🟡 MEDIUM — Placeholder Dates in Acceptance Criteria

**Severity: MEDIUM**
