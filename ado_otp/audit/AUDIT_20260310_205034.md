## 5. Findings

### Finding 18: 🔴 CRITICAL — Extreme Over-Commitment (39+ SP vs. 28 hrs Capacity)

**Severity: CRITICAL** | **New Finding**

With the migration of 9 stranded stories from Iteration 6.4 into 6.5, the iteration now carries **14 User Stories totaling 39+ Story Points** against only **28 hours of capacity** (Grace at 2 hrs/day × 14 days).

**Historical Context:**
- Iteration 6.4 committed ~41 SP with 42 hours capacity and delivered only **9 SP (22%)**
- Iteration 6.5 now commits ~39 SP with **33% less capacity** (28 hours)
- At the team's demonstrated velocity (9 SP per iteration), the realistic expectation is **6–9 SP** for this iteration

This represents a **~4.3x to 6.5x overcommitment** relative to demonstrated velocity. Per SAFe, teams should plan capacity-based iterations and use yesterday's weather (velocity) for forecasting. This commitment pattern virtually guarantees another significant under-delivery.

**Recommended Actions:**
1. Conduct an **emergency scope negotiation** to reduce committed SP to 8–10 SP maximum.
2. Identify 6–8 stories to return to the Product Backlog for future iteration consideration.
3. Prioritize the highest-value stories that align with the reduced 28-hour capacity.
4. External dependency items (Visa stories #198759, #198760, #198762) could be moved to the backlog if they are blocked on external parties.
