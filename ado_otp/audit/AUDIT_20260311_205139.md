## 11. Learnings & Improvement Patterns

### 11.1 What's Working (Amplify)

- **Persistent audit findings drive remediation.** #199353 was resolved after 7 audits. The audit cadence creates accountability.
- **Task closure breadth.** 4 stories saw task closures in a single day, suggesting Grace is context-switching effectively across the portfolio.
- **Implicit prioritization.** By leaving #200686 and #200707 in New state, the team is de facto managing WIP without formal deprioritization.

### 11.2 What Needs Attention (Improve)

- **Remaining medium-severity findings.** #199524's 3/12 deadline is due tomorrow — needs monitoring. #178753 is approaching its 1-year anniversary without closure.
