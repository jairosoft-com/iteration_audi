- No updates during the final days of Iteration 6.4
- No updates on Day 1 (Mar 9) or Day 2 (Mar 10) of Iteration 6.5
- No response to the P0 recommendations in the March 9 audit

**Hypothesis:** The team may be performing work outside ADO (email, chat, manual processes) without updating their board. This creates a dangerous gap between actual work and tracked work.

### 7.2 Pattern: Chronic Carryover

Items from PI 4 (September 2025) are still sitting on the active backlog without resolution. This indicates:

- No systematic backlog pruning process
- Items may no longer be relevant but consume cognitive load
- The team lacks a "Definition of Done" for Spikes that would force closure

### 7.3 Pattern: Spike Accumulation Without Conversion

The Spike-heavy backlog (64%) persists from the March 9 audit. Spikes are meant to be time-boxed research activities that produce actionable Stories. The absence of any User Stories suggests Spike findings are not being formalized into deliverable work.

With only **12 working days left** in Iteration 6.5 and zero planned work beyond a single Defect, the probability of meaningful PI6 delivery is very low. The IP iteration (6.6) should be used for planning PI7, not catching up on unfinished 6.5 work.
