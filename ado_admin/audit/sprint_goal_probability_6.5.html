Model Methodology

The Sprint Goal Probability is computed using a 5-factor weighted model evaluated daily: Burndown Pace (30%) compares actual vs ideal SP completion rate. Velocity Sufficiency (25%) assesses whether observed throughput can deliver remaining work. WIP Health (20%) measures what fraction of remaining work is actively in progress. Historical Performance (10%) uses the team's prior iteration track record (6.4: 100% completion). Capacity Buffer (15%) evaluates remaining hours vs estimated effort for remaining SP. The weighted score is scaled to a realistic probability range (40-95%).
