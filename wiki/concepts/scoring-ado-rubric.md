# ADO SAFe Audit — 7-Dimension Rubric

Canonical definition of how ADO teams are scored in this portfolio. Overall score = arithmetic mean of the seven dimensions.

## The seven dimensions

| # | Dimension | What it measures |
|---|-----------|------------------|
| 1 | Iteration Planning | Sprint goal, scope lock, story readiness at planning |
| 2 | Team Capacity | Capacity configured per iteration; load vs. available points |
| 3 | Estimation | Story-point coverage; estimate-to-actual drift |
| 4 | DoR Compliance | % of committed items meeting Definition of Ready |
| 5 | Work Item Balance | Feature / Story / Bug / Spike / Enabler distribution |
| 6 | Backlog Refinement | Groomed depth vs. next 1–2 iterations |
| 7 | Delivery Predictability | Committed vs. completed story points; close-day slope |
