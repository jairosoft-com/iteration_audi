## 6. Root Cause Hypotheses

Based on the data, the following root causes may explain the iteration failure:

1. **Resource constraint / availability** — With all work assigned to a single team member, any impediment (PTO, reassignment, competing priorities) blocks the entire iteration.
2. **No sprint goal or commitment ceremony** — Work may have been assigned to the iteration without team agreement during iteration planning.
3. **Lack of daily standup or impediment tracking** — Zero state changes across 14 days suggests no daily progress check-ins occurred.
4. **Iteration planning gap** — Items may have been backlog-groomed but not truly committed for the sprint.
